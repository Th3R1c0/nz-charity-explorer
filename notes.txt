i built a website to visulize financial records of over 28,000 charities in new zealand

kia ora, im a kiwi comp sci major at auckland uni and i thought ill share a website i built to show the financial records of every registered charity in new zealand. 
you can view detailed information of over 28,000 charities and thier  assets, expenditure, donations, goverment grants, officers, and more.
This data is publically avalible on the charities register but it only displays a small amount of information, and it is not easy to navigate visually on mobile. 
i made sure to incldue usefull financial metrics and visualizations for financial transpareny and ease of navigation on my website.
im a comp sci major at auckland university and i built this as one of my technical projects to put on my resume as im looking for a local internship to work as a developer, but i realised mabey people would like to use it so i bought a domain and made it public.
my iwi is ngati whatua, so it was pretty interesting browsing their financial records, seeing how rich they are lol.
its  still a work in progress, so I’d love to hear your feedback or any feature requests.


for nerds: 
techstack is react, typescript, tailwindcss, shadcn for frontend library , vercel for hosting, and some python scripts to sort data i hosted on google cloud run. 
this is definitly my least technical project i would usually got a more heavier stack like nextjs with more batteries included, but i had fun building it.
